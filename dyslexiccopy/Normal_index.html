I am a student at Coventry University studying Digital Media.

My purpose is to help parents and teachers to learn about Dyslexia: to understand it, to value it and to support it.

Our Mission

Raising awareness and educating parents and teachers about dyslexia is a major challenge, particularly given the serious global challenges they face. To play its part, I developed the Dysleksia website, which is aimed at parents, as well as a range of educational tools for students and teachers focusing on training and guiding parents of dyslexic children and giving more knowledge on the assistance that parents can provide for their children.
